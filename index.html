About the Festival

Tramore's best
food & outdoor celebration

A warm welcome awaits at the 2026 Vitamin Sea Festival on Midsummer weekend! Join us by the coast for a celebration of flavour, fresh sea air, and good company. From local bites to ocean delights, there's something to savour at every turn.

Award-Winning Local Producers and Eateries

Huge variety of food and produce showcasing the very best of Waterford and the South East.

Wild Swimming & Wellness Activities

Park running, kayaking, paddleboarding, sauna, yoga, carving, dancing, wellness workshops.

Live Music and Entertainment

Many varities of music, from traditional Irish to folk, and contemporary acts to keep the festival spirit alive.

Sustainability Commitment

We promote sustainable event practices by supporting local producers, encouraging reusable and recyclable materials, reducing waste, utilising renewable hydro-powered energy where available, and celebrating the natural coastal environment that makes Tramore so special.

About Taste Tramore

Taste Tramore

Taste Tramore is a voluntary, committee-led collective of local food and drink businesses, established in 2019 to champion Tramore, Co. Waterford as a leading culinary destination. Now in its seventh year, Taste Tramore has grown into a vibrant and collaborative network, driven by a passionate board drawn from the local hospitality and business community.

From cafés and restaurants to chefs and artisan producers, Taste Tramore brings together the very best of the town's food scene. Rooted in Tramore's coastal heritage, the initiative celebrates local flavours while fostering a strong sense of community pride.

Working in partnership with Waterford City & County Council, Taste Tramore plays a key role in promoting Tramore as a dynamic, year-round destination for food lovers and visitors alike.

A highlight of the calendar is the Vitamin Sea Festival—a unique celebration of food, culture, and community that showcases the people and produce that make Tramore special.

Through collaboration, events, and shared vision, Taste Tramore continues to support local businesses and invite visitors to experience the authentic flavours of the coast.
